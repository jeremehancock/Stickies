How it works

  • Add — double-click the board, or hit + Note.
  • Type — click a note and start writing.
  • Move — just drag it. It lifts, tilts and settles back.
  • Change Color — hover a note and tap a color dot.
  • Delete — hover a note and hit ×.
  • Undo a delete — tap Undo on the toast, or press Ctrl/⌘+Z.

About storage: notes live only in this browser via localStorage. They are not synced, shared between devices, or backed up — clearing your browser data (or opening a private window) starts you with a clean board. Treat this as a scratchpad for ideas, not long-term storage.

Clear the board?

This removes all notes and can't be undone.
